P R O F I L E

As a User Experience Designer, my main focus is understanding the wants and needs of my clients, and translating them into a clear, consistent and enjoyable user experience.

I started my career in Interior Design 10 years ago. Although it might seem like a world away from UX Design, for me it has proven to be an invaluable experience. I’ve learned from the very start of my career how the key to every successful design starts with listening to my clients’ goals and vision. Since my transitioning to UX Design, 5 years ago, I’ve been building on that knowledge and adding new skills to my portfolio. My main goal is still to provide the best experience a user can have with the product, but now I do this by executing Wireframes, Interface Designs, Interactive Prototypes, Personas, User Journeys, Process Flows and Sitemaps, and by conducting User Research and Usability Testing.

I continually strive to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in my field and improve my skills as a Ux designer, while maintaining the high standards of quality, that I set for myself.
