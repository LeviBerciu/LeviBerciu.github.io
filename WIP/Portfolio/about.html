About me


I'm an interdisciplinary designer with a focus on digital product design. My background spans UX, UI, motion design, 3D, and a bit of code—so I move easily between pixels, prototypes, and design systems. I’m passionate about creating thoughtful, intuitive digital experiences that make complex ideas feel simple, whether it's an app, a dashboard, or something entirely new.


I originally studied Cinematography, drawn to the power of visual storytelling. While earning my degree, I worked in interior design, but my curiosity about technology led me into the world of digital design—where creativity and innovation constantly intersect.


Outside of work, I enjoy diving into projects beyond my core focus, especially in physical product design and game design. I believe fresh perspectives come from stepping outside your lane once in a while.
