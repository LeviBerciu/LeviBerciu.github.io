Printmaking Tool

MINI B.B.B.


A “Ball Bearing Baren” is a disk-like hand tool which suspends stainless steel balls. The balls rotate freely and deliver multiple pressure points evenly across the disc. It is used in block printing to burnish the back of a sheet of paper, lifting ink from the block.


I enjoy lino printing as a hobby, but my studio space is very limited. I designed and 3D printed the “Mini B.B.B.” to serve as a temporary alternative to a traditional printing press.
